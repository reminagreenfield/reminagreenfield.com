Remina Greenfield

I am a New York-based researcher and artist interested in the hybrid possibilities emerging between living things and computational systems. I have worked in a professional capacity as Director of Research for the conceptual artist Anicka Yi for over four years. In recent years I have also been a member of the NEW INC Creative Science track, taught original graduate courses in the Design and Technology department at Parsons School of Design, produced a solo exhibition at Pratt Institute, and published several articles and interviews on aesthetics and contemporary art.
