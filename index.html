Remina Greenfield

I am a New York-based researcher and artist interested in the hybrid possibilities emerging between living things and computational systems. My collaborative explorations synthesize expert knowledge of engineers, artists, scientists, art fabricators and conservators. In a professional reseach capacity, I have worked as Director of Research at Anicka Yi Studio for over four years, providing critical research and development for projects ranging from kelp sculptures to autonomous floating machines to paintings made through machine learning. I hold an MFA in Design and Technology from Parsons School of Design where I have also designed and taught several original graduate courses as a part-time faculty member. I participate as a community member of NEW INC, a New Museum-led cultural incubator dedicated to supporting innovation, collaboration, and entrepreneurship across art, design, and technology.
