Remina Greenfield

I am a New York-based researcher and artist interested in the hybrid possibilities emerging between living things and computational systems. I have worked in a professional capacity as Director of Research at Anicka Yi Studio for over four years. In this capacity, I've provided critical research and development for projects such as In Love With The World At Tate Modern, a TED talk about Intelligent floating machines inspired by nature, Metaspore at Pirelli HangarBicocca, and Venice Biennale 2019

In recent years I have also been a member of the NEW INC Creative Science track, taught original graduate courses in the Design and Technology department at Parsons School of Design, produced a solo exhibition at Pratt Institute, and published several articles and interviews on aesthetics and contemporary art.
